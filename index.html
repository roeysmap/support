About RoeysMap

RoeysMap is a memorial app celebrating Roey's life, travels, and the places that meant the most to him. Explore an interactive map of his journeys, read his story, and join a community of friends and family who share memories, photos, and check-ins from meaningful locations around the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I explore Roey's places?

Open the app and go to the Map tab. You'll see pins marking Roey's documented places as well as community-contributed stickers. Tap any pin to view details, memories, photos, and comments left by others.

How do I add my own memory or sticker?

You can contribute by adding a community sticker to the map, writing a memory on a place's detail page, or leaving a comment. Use the location picker to find the right spot and attach photos, videos, or audio notes.

Why does the app need location access?

Location access is used to center the map on your current position and help you find nearby places. Your location data is never shared with third parties.

The map isn't loading — what should I do?

Make sure your device has an active internet connection. If the issue persists, close and reopen the app. You can also try restarting your device or reinstalling the app from the App Store.

How do I read Roey's story?

Tap the "Roey's Story" section from the app's navigation. There you'll find a multi-page biography covering his childhood, travels, and life philosophy, along with a photo timeline.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Send us an email using the contact button below. Please describe the issue or idea in as much detail as possible, including your device model and iOS version if reporting a bug.
